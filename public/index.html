MELANGE is an annual Inter Collegiate Techno-Cultural festival which aims at encouraging students to share their ideas. MELANGE is a two day cultural fest with over 50 events including technical, sports, gaming, etc. At MELANGE, we have a number of classified competitions, The most liked for meeting students’ capacities. The theme for this year’s MELANGE being SANGHATHAN VA VEERTA. We are excited to inform that MELANGE 2020 will be organized at AMC Engineering College, Bengaluru during 17th & 18th Of April 2020.

AMC Engineering College

AMC stands for 27 years of technical academic excellence and also certified for quality education by NBA through AICTE Accreditation and Permanently Affiliated to VTU University.

A team of highly committed and qualified Engineering professors, progressively producing best results between 90-100% along with 8-10 VTU university rank holders every year.

Unique Teaching Learning Process for UG programs with 52 acres of Wi-Fi enabled campus. A complete cricket stadium inside the college campus in collaboration with Karnataka State Cricket Association.

A complete ERP solution for student admissions, college administrations, which also helps the parents to monitor student’s examination results, teacher’s remarks, continuous progress of scholar and technological aspects.
